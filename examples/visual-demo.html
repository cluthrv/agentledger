How It Works
🔗 Hash Chain
Each record's SHA-256 hash includes the previous record's hash. Modify any record → the chain breaks → tampering is instantly detected.
🌳 Merkle Tree
All record hashes form a binary tree. The root hash is a single fingerprint for the entire session. Verify any record in O(log n).
💡 Why This Matters

Without AgentLedger: "The AI set the price to $150,375." No proof of how or why. Logs can be edited.

With AgentLedger: Cryptographic proof of every step — what the agent queried, what rules it applied, what it calculated — and proof nothing was altered.
